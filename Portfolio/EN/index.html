About me

Hello, my name is Marc, I am 18 years old, I was born and live in Vilafranca del Penedés, where I also did my compulsory primary education and compulsory secondary education, each in different schools, my primary school was Estalella i Graells, while my school secondary school was Sant Josep. When I finished compulsory secondary school, I decided to do a SIMIX intermediate degree in Barcelona, in the same center where I am studying a higher degree in computer science (DAW), at the STUCOM educational center, located on Pl. Cataluña in Barcelona, to finish, after finishing higher education, start working (most likely) or go to university.

I chose this higher degree, since my interest is to end up as a Web Developer working, creating, modifying and/or improving web pages.

I have always been a person interested and passionate about the computer world. From a very young age, I was struck by how those sites were created in which I could navigate, how one page magically led to another and were interconnected apparently by nothing, which was what made all that information come together in the same place and as that web page design was created. Because of that, my interest in web pages grew and my admiration for the people who created them grew, thus deciding to want to be part of this world of web development.

Apart from this computer science degree, I became interested in the multi-sports degree, since I have always done some sport since I can remember, I started as a child doing swimming and soccer, I went through athletics, climbing, human towers, among other sports, thanks to that My physical condition has always been suitable for exercising.

To this day no longer play football but I am passionate about sports, I have worked as a referee with children from 1st to 4th grade, I have helped as a monitor at sports events, and I have worked as a coach for children between 3 and 5 years old, for what got me interested in that middle cycle.

Finally, I decided to join the computer science degree, since just as I have always played sports, I have always been interested in computer science and programming, my parents are computer scientists and my brother also decided to go down this path, but they all specialized in cybersecurity, and as I said before, I am passionate about the idea of being a web developer, so I ended up doing DAW and not ASIX.

So I am here to learn. For that, I would be interested in being able to study while working or doing internships, to gain real experience with which I can train to learn about this world, not only in school with internships, but in a work environment in which I can learn with others and by myself to be able to develop my knowledge and cover everything I don't know until now.

As this is one of my first times in this work environment, I would like to develop the role of apprentice or assistant to someone who is interested in teaching me how everything works, and over time go from apprentice to prepared worker.
